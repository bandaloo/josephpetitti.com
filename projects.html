HCI PROJECT 5

HCI Project 5 is a group project for CS 3041, Human Computer Interactions. The project was used to explore the technology of affective computing, using technology to promote health and wellbeing. We used Affectiva's emotion-detecting API. The program analyzes your expression through your webcam to see which of two options you like more. To see how it works, click here.

HCI PROJECT 5a

HCI Project 5a is a variation of HCI Project 5. In this version, instead of determining which of two options you like more, it shows you ten images of dogs and detects which one you like the most. Click here to see it work.

HCI PROJECT 5b

HCI Project 5b is another variation of HCI Project 5. This time, it helps you decide what to make for dinner. It shows you ten images of food, and tracks your emotions while looking at them. Then it shows you the recipe for whichever one you smiled at the most. To try it out, click here.
